## 🛡️ Administrative Objective
This memo records the formal decision made after reviewing the WRH pilot record. It determines whether WRH should continue, revise, pause, repeat the pilot, or expand. The memo is based on documented implementation data, facilitator fidelity records, safety logs, support bridge performance, participant feedback, and site readiness accuracy.

This memo does **not** make clinical outcome claims. It does not determine whether WRH treated trauma, reduced symptoms, prevented suicide, or produced therapeutic effects.

## 7. Claims Boundary
This decision memo supports only operational conclusions. It does not support clinical claims.

WRH may use this memo to state operational findings such as:

*   Whether WRH was delivered with fidelity
*   Whether participants engaged without required disclosure
*   Whether activation events were documented
*   Whether the Closed-Loop Support Bridge functioned as designed
*   Whether the Site Readiness Gate accurately predicted site performance
*   Whether revisions are required before expansion

WRH may **not** use this memo to claim:

*   Clinical effectiveness
*   PTSD or complex PTSD symptom reduction
*   Suicide prevention outcomes
*   Addiction treatment outcomes
*   Therapeutic benefit
*   Replacement of licensed clinical care
