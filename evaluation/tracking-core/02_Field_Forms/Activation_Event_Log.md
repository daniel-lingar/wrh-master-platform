## 🛡️ Administrative Objective
This log documents participant activation events during WRH pilot sessions. It records observable facts, response level, actions taken, and whether the Closed-Loop Support Bridge was activated. This form does **not** diagnose, interpret, or clinically assess the participant.

---

## ⚠️ Response Level Observed
Mark the highest response level reached during the event.

*   **[  ] GREEN** — Participant regulated and able to continue.
*   **[  ] YELLOW** — Participant activated but stable; facilitator slowed pace, grounded the room, or offered a break.
*   **[  ] ORANGE** — Participant could not return to baseline, requested help, appeared dissociated, expressed hopelessness, or showed distress beyond normal group activation. Curriculum paused for that participant and support lead was contacted.
*   **[  ] RED** — Immediate safety concern present. WRH stopped and agency crisis protocol activated.

> When in doubt, record the more conservative level. It is never wrong to slow down or pause. It is never acceptable to push through visible distress.
