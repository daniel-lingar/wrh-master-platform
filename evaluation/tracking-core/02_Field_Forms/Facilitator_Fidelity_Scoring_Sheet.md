## 🛡️ Administrative Objective
This scoring instrument is used by an independent reviewer or supervisor to audit a live WRH session. Its sole purpose is to verify that the facilitator strictly enforces group boundaries and delivers the structured educational content without drifting into clinical therapy, personal advice-giving, or trauma-disclosure solicitation.

---

## 📊 Fidelity Evaluation Criteria
The evaluator will mark **YES** if the condition was met, or **NO** if a boundary drift or structural failure occurred.

### 1. Structural Adherence
*   **[ YES / NO ]** The facilitator opened the session on time and clearly stated the specific session topic and goals.
*   **[ YES / NO ]** The facilitator delivered the curriculum points using the plain-language educational scripts provided.
*   **[ YES / NO ]** The facilitator closed the session with the designated grounding summary.

### 2. Boundary Enforcement (Critical Safety Items)
*   **[ YES / NO ]** The facilitator explicitly reminded participants of the strict non-disclosure rule regarding personal historical stories.
*   **[ YES / NO ]** **[CRITICAL]** If a participant began detailing a specific historical trauma event, the facilitator immediately executed the mandatory "Stop and Re-center" transition script.
*   **[ YES / NO ]** The facilitator completely refrained from diagnosing, analyzing, or giving clinical mental health advice to any individual.

### 3. Group Management
*   **[ YES / NO ]** The facilitator maintained an objective, educational tone throughout the session.
*   **[ YES / NO ]** The facilitator did not solicit deep emotional processing or historical trauma narratives from the group.
