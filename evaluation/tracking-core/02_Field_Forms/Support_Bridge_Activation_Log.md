## 🛡️ Administrative Objective
This log documents every activation of the WRH Closed-Loop Support Bridge. Its purpose is to verify that the participant was not simply referred out, but was connected, stabilized, transferred to the agency pathway, or documented and escalated as unresolved according to approved site procedures.

This form does **not** record clinical diagnosis, therapy content, or private trauma history.
