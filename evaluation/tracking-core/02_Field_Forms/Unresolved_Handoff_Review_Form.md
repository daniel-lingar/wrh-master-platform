## 🛡️ Administrative Objective
This review form is used when a WRH support bridge activation remains unresolved. Its purpose is to document what prevented confirmed connection, what follow-up occurred, whether the agency escalation pathway functioned, and what corrective action is required before continued or expanded deployment.

This form is operational and administrative. It does **not** contain diagnosis, clinical assessment, therapy notes, or personal trauma history.
