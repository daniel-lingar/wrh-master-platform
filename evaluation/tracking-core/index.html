Purpose

This repository is the pilot-operating layer for WRH. It is not the curriculum source, not the buyer packet, and not a clinical treatment record. It contains the forms and evaluation tools needed to run a controlled WRH pilot, capture what happens, review site performance, and decide what must change before continuation or expansion.

Core standard: A pilot is not a performance. A pilot is a stress test.

Claims Boundary

May Support

  • Operational delivery findings
  • Facilitator fidelity findings
  • Participant engagement without required disclosure
  • Support Bridge performance
  • Unresolved handoff review
  • Site readiness accuracy
  • Required revisions before expansion

Does Not Support

  • Clinical effectiveness claims
  • PTSD or C-PTSD treatment claims
  • Suicide prevention outcome claims
  • Addiction treatment claims
  • Symptom reduction claims
  • Replacement of licensed care
