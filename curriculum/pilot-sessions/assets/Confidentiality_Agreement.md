# WRH Pilot Program: Confidentiality Agreement

**Purpose:** This Confidentiality Agreement outlines the expectations for maintaining privacy and respect within the "What Really Happened" (WRH) Pilot Program. The success and safety of this program rely on the commitment of all participants to uphold these principles.

**Agreement:**

By participating in the WRH Pilot Program, I, the undersigned, agree to the following:

1.  **Confidentiality of Shared Information:** I understand that during group sessions and discussions, participants may share personal and sensitive information. I agree to keep all such information strictly confidential. This means I will not disclose any details shared by other participants outside of the program setting, including their identities, stories, or personal circumstances.

2.  **Anonymity Outside the Program:** I agree to maintain the anonymity of other participants outside of the program. I will not approach or acknowledge other participants in public settings unless they initiate contact and indicate their comfort with doing so. I will not discuss their participation in the program with anyone outside of the program.

3.  **Respectful Engagement:** I agree to engage with all participants and facilitators in a respectful and non-judgmental manner. I understand that diverse perspectives and experiences will be shared, and I commit to fostering a safe and supportive environment for everyone.

4.  **Personal Responsibility for Disclosure:** I understand that while the program encourages sharing, I am solely responsible for what I choose to disclose about myself. I will consider my own comfort level and boundaries before sharing personal information.

5.  **Limits of Confidentiality:** I understand that there are legal and ethical limits to confidentiality. If a participant discloses an intent to harm themselves or others, or if there is evidence of child or elder abuse, the facilitators are legally and ethically obligated to report this information to the appropriate authorities. In such cases, every effort will be made to discuss this with the participant beforehand.

6.  **No Recording:** I agree not to record, photograph, or otherwise document any part of the program sessions (audio, video, or written) without the explicit written consent of all participants and facilitators involved.

7.  **Consequences of Breach:** I understand that a breach of this Confidentiality Agreement may result in my removal from the WRH Pilot Program to ensure the safety and trust of all participants.

I have read and understand the terms of this Confidentiality Agreement and agree to abide by them throughout my participation in the "What Really Happened" Pilot Program.
