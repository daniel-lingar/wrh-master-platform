**WHEN THE SYSTEM FIRES:**

1. **FEET** — Press both feet flat on the floor. Feel something solid.
2. **BREATHE** — Exhale longer than the inhale.
3. **NAME IT** — "My system is activated. I can pause before I act."
4. **WAIT** — Give the wave time to rise and fall.
5. **CHOOSE** — Take the next safest available step.
