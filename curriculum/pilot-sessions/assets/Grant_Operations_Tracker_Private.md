### Update Protocol
1. **Quarterly Review**: Conduct a full review of [grants.gov](https://www.grants.gov) and agency-specific portals.
2. **Priority Scoring**: Assign priority based on alignment with the 18-module framework and partner availability.
3. **Status Tracking**: Update status from *Researching* → *Pre-Draft* → *Active* → *Submitted*.
4. **Redaction**: Ensure all internal notes and deadlines are removed before exporting the public-facing `grant_match_matrix.pdf`.

### Internal Scoring Criteria
* **Alignment (1-5)**: How well the curriculum modules map to the NOFO requirements.
* **Partner Readiness (1-5)**: Availability of implementation partners for the specific grant.
* **Competitive Gap (1-5)**: Uniqueness of the Capitol Contracts LLC approach compared to existing solutions.
