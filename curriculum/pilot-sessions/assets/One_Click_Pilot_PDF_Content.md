## **3. THE WRH CURRICULUM: 3-ARC STRUCTURE**
The 30-session journey is divided into three logical arcs:

### **Arc 1: The Machine (Sessions 00–10)**
*How the system gets built into you.*  
Focus: The Hum, The Mask, The Crash, and The Loop.

### **Arc 2: The Drivers (Sessions 11–20)**
*What keeps the engine running.*  
Focus: Triggers vs. Activation, The Threshold, and Time Collapse.

### **Arc 3: The Interrupt & Restore (Sessions 21–30)**
*How you rewrite the code.*  
Focus: The Pattern Veto, Executive Function Restore, and The Code Rewrite.

## **4. RETENTION & SAFETY PROTOCOLS**
### **Beating the "Learn 20, Forget 80" Pattern**
- **20:80 Application Drills:** 80% of session time is dedicated to practical application.
- **Spaced Boosters:** Automated prompts at 1, 3, 7, and 30 days post-session.
- **One-Tool Commitment:** Practical focus on one specific tool between sessions.

### **Safety & Risk Management**
- **Yellow/Red Light Protocol:** Standardized room regulation and opt-out options.
- **Warm-Handoff Protocol:** Immediate connection to the **Veterans Crisis Line (988, press 1)** or on-site Stepping Stones clinical staff.
- **Non-Clinical Boundary:** WRH is psychoeducational, not therapeutic.
