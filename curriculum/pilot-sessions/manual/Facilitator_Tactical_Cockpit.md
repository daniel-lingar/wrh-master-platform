## **In-Room Tactical Tools**

### **1. Fidelity Checkpoints (Every 15–20 Minutes)**
- **Check 1:** Are you staying in WRH facilitator tone: calm, analytical, non-shaming, non-clinical, and unsentimental?
- **Check 2:** Is the room regulated? If not, pause content and use a grounding or reset script.
- **Check 3:** Are you following the session structure, facilitator actions, and script/cue tables instead of improvising clinical interpretation?

### **2. Quick-Dial Support**
- **Immediate Emergency:** Follow host-site emergency procedures or call local emergency services when required.
- **Crisis Support:** In the United States, call or text **988** for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
- **Veterans Crisis Line:** Veterans may call **988**, then press **1**.
- **On-site Support Contact:** Add the host site's approved staff contact before launch.
- **Warm-Handoff Protocol:** Use the [Risk Management Checklist](../checklists/Risk_Management_Checklist.md).
