## PART I — FACILITATOR INFRASTRUCTURE

### Facilitator Core Rules

| Rule | Execution |
|------|-----------|
| **PAUSE** | Count silently to 3 after any reframe or key line. The silence is doing work. |
| **DROP** | Lower your voice on the second half of every important sentence. |
| **BREATHE** | Take one visible, slow breath before the Rapid Reframes segment. |
| **EYES** | When asking a Mirror Moment question, lock on one person. Break eye contact first. |

### Hard Limits
- No more than 2 sessions back-to-back.
- You are not responsible for their healing. You are responsible for the container.
- This is not therapy. If a participant requires clinical intervention, activate the Red Light protocol.

### Safety Protocols

**Yellow Light (Mildly Activated)**  
Participant shows visible distress — shallow breathing, tearful, scanning exits.  
> *"Let's pause for a second. Everyone — feet flat on the floor. One slow breath out. We'll wait."*  
Do not name the participant. Let the group regulation do the work.

**Red Light (Flooded)**  
Participant is shaking, leaves the room, stops responding, or discloses active crisis.  
- Let them leave without explanation.
- Have co-facilitator or designated support person available.
- After session: Conduct 1:1 check-in using Plan B handout framework.
- If active safety concern: Follow mandatory reporting/crisis protocol.
