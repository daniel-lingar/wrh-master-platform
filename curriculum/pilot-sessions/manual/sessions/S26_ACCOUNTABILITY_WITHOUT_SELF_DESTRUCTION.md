"When he receives `feedback`, even if it's `constructive`, `The System` registers it as a `critical threat`. `The Hum` spikes, `freeze` engages, and he enters `The Shame Spiral`. "This `protocol` ensures he avoids `perceived punishment` by `shutting down`. But the cost is `growth` and `adaptation`. He cannot `optimize The System` if he cannot process `feedback` as `data`, rather than `judgment`. He is not `irresponsible`; he is `operating` on `old code` that dictates `avoidance` as the ultimate `defense`."
