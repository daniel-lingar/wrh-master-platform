## **2. ZERO-COST MISSION-ALIGNED INVESTMENT**
This pilot is structured as a **zero-cost partnership** for the 2026 launch phase. Capitol Contracts LLC provides the WRH curriculum materials, facilitator orientation support, and digital assets at no cost to Stepping Stones in exchange for structured implementation feedback.

Expected pilot feedback may include:

- **Delivery Observations:** Notes on pacing, clarity, room safety, and participant engagement.
- **Outcome Data:** De-identified retention, attendance, tool recall, and tool-application metrics where available.
- **Qualitative Feedback:** De-identified participant and staff observations approved by Stepping Stones before any external use.
- **Revision Guidance:** Recommendations for adapting WRH to residential recovery environments.

No participant is required to provide a testimonial, disclose personal history, or permit public use of their story.

---

## **3. RESIDENTIAL RECOVERY INTEGRATION**
- **Scheduling:** Sessions are integrated into the Stepping Stones residential routine in a way that avoids conflict with existing clinical, pastoral, peer-support, work-therapy, or recovery-program requirements.
- **Peer Pods:** Participants may be grouped into peer pods to reinforce tool recall and practical application between sessions, aligning with the Stepping Stones "Strength in Community" value.
- **Voluntary Participation:** While sessions may be integrated into the schedule, engagement remains non-disclosure-based. Participants may pass, remain silent, step out, or engage through written reflection according to site procedures.

---

## **4. RETENTION & OUTCOMES TRACKING**
This pilot uses repetition and application-based learning to improve recall and real-world transfer.

- **Spaced Boosters:** Optional reminders or review prompts at 1, 3, 7, and 30 days after key sessions.
- **20:80 Application Design:** Sessions prioritize a small amount of core content followed by practical examples, review, and application.
- **One-Tool Commitment:** Participants are encouraged, but not required, to practice one specific WRH tool, such as `The Pattern Veto`, between sessions.

---

## **5. SAFETY & RISK MANAGEMENT**
- **Yellow/Red Light Protocol:** Standardized room-regulation procedure as defined in the [Safety Rules](../checklists/Safety_and_Participation_Rules.md).
- **Warm-Handoff Protocol:** If a participant’s needs exceed the WRH educational scope, facilitators route the situation to on-site Stepping Stones leadership, licensed staff, established site procedures, crisis resources, or emergency services as appropriate.
- **Crisis Resource Awareness:** In the United States, participants experiencing suicidal thoughts or crisis may call or text **988**. Veterans may call **988 and press 1** for the Veterans Crisis Line.
- **Non-Clinical Boundary:** WRH is psychoeducational, not therapeutic. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, crisis intervention, or clinical processing.
