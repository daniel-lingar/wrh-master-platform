# **MOBILE PLAN B CARD**
## **HIGH-CONTRAST PARTICIPANT SUPPORT VIEW**
**Tone:** Immediate, Direct, Action-Oriented

---

## **1. THE RESET (DO THIS NOW)**
1.  **FEET FLAT:** Press your feet into the floor. Feel the weight.
2.  **LONG EXHALE:** Breathe in slowly. Exhale longer than the inhale.
3.  **REPEAT:** Do it again. Slow in. Longer out.

---

## **2. THE NAME (IDENTIFY THE SYSTEM)**
- **"THIS MAY BE THE HUM."**
- **"THIS MAY BE THE MASK FALLING."**
- **"THIS MAY BE THE CRASH."**
- **"THIS MAY BE THE FREEZE."**

Naming the pattern is not a diagnosis. It is a pause point.

---

## **3. THE ACTION (CHOOSE ONE)**
- **STEP OUT:** Leave the room according to site procedure. No explanation needed.
- **SAY "YELLOW":** Signal that you are at the edge.
- **SAY "RED":** Signal that you are done participating and need support.
- **FIND STAFF:** If you do not feel safe, contact approved site staff immediately.

---

## **4. SUPPORT BOUNDARY**
This card is an educational support tool. It is not therapy, medical care, crisis monitoring, or emergency response.

If you are in immediate danger, follow site procedures, contact approved staff, or call local emergency services.

In the United States, call or text **988** for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Veterans may call **988 and press 1**.
