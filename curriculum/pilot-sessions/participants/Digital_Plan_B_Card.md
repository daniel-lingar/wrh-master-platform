# WRH Digital Plan B Card
*Quick-access tool for hyperarousal and focus problems.*

---

## **If you feel terror or can't focus right now:**

### **1. Ground Once**
Put both feet flat on the floor. Take one slow, deliberate exhale. Feel the weight of your body in the chair.

### **2. Drop Performance**
You are not here to impress anyone. You don't have to look like you're "doing group" right. You are allowed to just be in the room.

### **3. Catch One Sentence**
Don't try to track the whole 75 minutes. Just listen for **one idea** or **one sentence** that makes sense to you today. Ignore the rest.

### **4. Write One Word**
Later today, write down one word about that sentence. That is enough. You have successfully completed this session.

---

> *"If you never say a word, but you keep showing up and quietly stealing one sentence per session, this program is working for you."*
