# WRH 30-Day Retention Booster Sign-up
*Optional spaced-repetition prompts for pilot participants.*

---

## **Why Sign Up?**
WRH uses spaced repetition to help participants remember simple tools after the session ends. Optional retention boosters provide low-pressure reminders connected to concepts such as the Pattern Veto, Somatic Inventory, Mirror Moment, Plan B View, and the Hum.

The goal is not to track private trauma information. The goal is to help participants remember and practice educational tools between sessions.

### **What Participants May Receive:**
- **Day 1:** A quick reminder of one WRH tool.
- **Day 3:** A short prompt to notice body signals or stress patterns.
- **Day 7:** A low-pressure reflection question.
- **Day 30:** An optional 1-minute follow-up survey about tool recall.

---

## **How to Sign Up**

### **Option 1: Digital Portal**
If a WRH web portal is active for the pilot site, participants may opt in through the approved portal process.

### **Option 2: SMS/Text**
If SMS boosters are approved for a pilot site, the host organization will provide the official opt-in number and instructions before launch.

No participant should text personal information, trauma history, medical details, legal details, or crisis information into the booster system.

---

## **Privacy & Choice**
- **Voluntary:** Participants may opt out at any time according to the approved pilot-site process.
- **Minimal Information:** Booster systems should collect only the information needed to deliver reminders and track de-identified pilot metrics.
- **No Trauma Disclosure:** Participants are not asked to share personal trauma history, clinical details, or private case information.
- **Non-Therapeutic:** These are educational prompts, not therapy, clinical monitoring, crisis care, or medical advice.
- **Crisis Boundary:** Booster prompts are not monitored as an emergency service. Participants in crisis should contact local emergency services, site staff, or call/text **988** in the United States.
