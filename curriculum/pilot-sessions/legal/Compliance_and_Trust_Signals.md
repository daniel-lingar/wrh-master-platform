## **Non-Clinical Disclaimer**
The **What Really Happened (WRH) Psychoeducation Program** is strictly **psychoeducational and non-therapeutic**.

- **Not Therapy:** WRH is not a substitute for clinical mental health treatment, psychotherapy, medical advice, crisis care, or licensed professional services.
- **No Required Disclosure:** The program does not require participants to disclose, recount, or process personal trauma histories.
- **Educational Scope:** The curriculum focuses on pattern recognition, nervous-system adaptation, executive-function disruption, shame cycles, freeze responses, and practical interruption points.
- **No Diagnosis or Treatment:** Capitol Contracts LLC and WRH facilitators do not provide clinical diagnosis, treatment planning, medication guidance, or therapeutic intervention.
- **Existing Care Encouraged:** Participants are encouraged to maintain existing clinical, medical, peer-support, case-management, legal, or crisis-support relationships where applicable.

## **Safety & Warm-Handoff Protocols**
Although WRH is non-clinical, participant safety remains central to implementation.

- **Standardized Safety Boundaries:** Facilitators are instructed to avoid forced disclosure, clinical interpretation, confrontation, or trauma-processing exercises.
- **Escalation Awareness:** If a participant presents immediate safety concerns, facilitators route the situation to the host organization’s established safety procedures, licensed staff, emergency contacts, crisis services, or local emergency response as appropriate.
- **Warm-Handoff Orientation:** Facilitators are trained to connect participants with qualified providers or crisis resources when needs exceed the WRH educational scope.
- **Crisis Resource Awareness:** In the United States, participants experiencing suicidal thoughts or crisis may call or text **988**. Veterans may call **988 and press 1** for the Veterans Crisis Line.
- **Voluntary Engagement:** Participants retain choice in their level of engagement and may pause, step out, or opt out according to host-site procedures.
