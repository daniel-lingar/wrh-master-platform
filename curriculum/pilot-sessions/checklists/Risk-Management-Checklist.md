# Risk Management & Liability Mitigation Checklist
**Program:** What Really Happened (Trauma Psychoeducation)  
**Provider:** Capitol Contracts LLC  
**Status:** Non-Clinical, Peer-Facilitated Educational Program

---

## 1. Scope of Practice & Clear Boundaries
- [x] **Defined Non-Clinical Status:** All program materials explicitly state the program is "psychoeducation," not "therapy" or "treatment."
- [x] **Peer-Facilitator Model:** Roles are defined as facilitators, not clinicians, counselors, or medical professionals.
- [x] **No Diagnosis/Treatment:** Facilitators are prohibited from providing mental health diagnoses or clinical treatment recommendations.

## 2. Legal Disclaimers & Notifications
- [x] **Website Footer Disclaimer:** High-visibility notice on every page of the program website.
- [x] **Prominent Contact Disclaimer:** Strengthened red-box disclaimer placed immediately before the "Request Pilot" call-to-action.
- [x] **Document Disclaimers:** Every downloadable PDF (Capabilities Statement, Sample Sessions, Pricing) contains a full legal disclaimer on the first page and in the footer.
- [x] **Liability Waiver:** Language explicitly stating that Capitol Contracts LLC and facilitators assume no liability for participant outcomes.

## 3. Crisis & Safety Protocols
- [x] **Direct Questioning Protocol:** Standardized steps for facilitators to ask directly about suicidal ideation or intent.
- [x] **Risk Level Categorization:** Clear "High/Moderate/Low" risk levels with specific, mandated actions for each.
- [x] **Emergency Integration:** Mandated immediate contact with 911/Security for high-risk situations.
- [x] **988 Lifeline Integration:** Prominent placement of the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline in all facilitator and participant materials.
- [x] **Documentation Requirement:** Mandated incident reporting and supervisor notification within 1 hour of any crisis disclosure.

## 4. Facilitator Training & Support
- [x] **Scripted Curriculum:** All 18 sessions are fully scripted to prevent "off-book" clinical advice.
- [x] **Safety Check-Outs:** Every session includes a mandated safety check and grounding exercise.
- [x] **Self-Care & Debriefing:** Built-in protocols for facilitators to debrief with supervisors after difficult sessions to prevent secondary trauma.

## 5. Administrative & Operational Controls
- [x] **UEI & CAGE Registered:** Fully registered federal contractor (Capitol Contracts LLC).
- [x] **Pilot Outcome Tracking:** Standardized pre/post measures to monitor program impact and safety.
- [x] **Supervision Structure:** Requirement for facilitators to report to a designated program supervisor.

---
**Note for Insurers:** This checklist demonstrates a proactive approach to risk management by clearly defining the program's non-clinical boundaries and providing robust safety nets for high-risk populations.
