# CAPABILITY STATEMENT
**Capitol Contracts LLC**  
*Pioneering Psychoeducational Pattern Recognition & Regulation Training*

## Company Overview
Capitol Contracts LLC delivers innovative, evidence-based programs designed to address critical challenges within the veteran community and federal systems. Led by Daniel Lingar, we specialize in translating complex trauma responses into understandable, actionable patterns through structured, repeatable psychoeducational programs.

## Core Competencies: The Wounded Blueprint
Our core IP, the **Wounded Blueprint**, provides a session-by-session mapping of What Really Happened (WRH) to nervous-system patterns. We specialize in:

- **Program Design & Curriculum Development:** Serialized, 30-session programs (WRH Pilot Program) focusing on logical progression and pattern recognition.
- **Risk Mitigation & Liability Reduction:** Robust safety protocols (Plan B Protocol) reducing liability and increasing participant retention.
- **System Logic Translation:** Articulating complex psychological responses (freeze, dissociation) as engineering problems, not character flaws.
- **Measurable Outcomes:** Quantifiable behavioral changes and risk reduction aligned with federal funding priorities (e.g., lowering veteran suicide rates).
- **Facilitator Training & Standardization:** Standardized, replicable curricula enabling delivery by trained facilitators for scalability.

## Narrative Depth & Advocacy
- **Outlaw Gospel:** Narrative advocacy for the marginalized and trauma-impacted.
- **Trauma-Informed Courts Act Concept:** Legislative framework for trauma-aware judicial processes.

## Ancillary Technical & Vocational Solutions
We provide technical and vocational solutions directly tied to reentry and vocational skills development, including:
